Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the app (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects your information. It outlines what data may be gathered, why it is necessary, how it is stored, and under what circumstances it may be shared. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your data is handled. By using the App, you acknowledge and agree to the terms described in this policy.

Information Collected

The App may collect the following types of information:

  • User Preferences: Stored locally using SharedPreferences or Jetpack DataStore (e.g., alarm settings, theme, notifications).
  • Device Information: Such as device model, OS version, screen size, language, and unique device identifier (used for crash reporting or analytics).
  • Internet Usage: The App may check internet connectivity and fetch remote configuration or content as needed.
  • Ad Interaction Data: Anonymous data collected by third-party ad providers to improve ad relevance and experience.
  • App Usage Data: Anonymous events (e.g., button clicks, screen visits) may be collected for analytics and user experience improvements.

Permissions

The app may request certain permissions depending on the features you choose to use:

  • Location (Approximate or Precise): Required for direction and location-based functionality.
  • Camera: Required for features such as scanning or capturing images.
  • Storage Access: Used for saving content or selecting images from your device.
  • Internet: Required to fetch data, display advertisements, or check connectivity.
  • Notification Access: To send reminders, alarms, or important updates.
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback or silent alerts.

Permissions are requested only when required and can be managed in your device's system settings. If a permission is denied, related features may be limited or unavailable.

Use of Information

The collected information may be used to:

  • Provide and improve the App’s core features (e.g., alarms, reminders).
  • Store and remember your preferences.
  • Enhance app performance, stability, and personalization.
  • Support development through analytics and ad revenue.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information may be shared only:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of users or the public.
  • With third-party services such as Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics (see Third-Party Services).

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services such as:

  • Google AdMob (ads and monetization)
  • Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics (analytics and crash reporting)

Notifications

The App may send local notifications to inform you of alarms, events, or other time-based reminders. You can control notification settings from within the App or your device.

Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or storage system is fully secure.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If a parent believes their child has provided personal data, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

User data is stored as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, or as required by law. Analytics and crash data are anonymized and retained for service improvement.

Advertising

The app may display advertisements provided by third-party networks such as Google AdMob. Depending on your region and choices, ads may be personalized based on device information and app usage data. You can control ad preferences through your device settings or opt-out via the ad provider’s privacy tools.

Your Choices and Rights

You can manage permissions from your device settings. You may also request access to, correction, or deletion of any personal data stored (if applicable) by contacting us via email. If you choose not to grant certain permissions, some features of the app may be limited.

Analytics

The app may use anonymized analytics tools to understand usage patterns, such as which screens are visited or which features are most used. This data helps improve usability and performance over time.

Error and Crash Reporting

The app may automatically collect anonymized crash reports and error logs through third-party services to help diagnose problems and improve stability. This data does not include personally identifiable information.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page and, where appropriate, notified within the App.
