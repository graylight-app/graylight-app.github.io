Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the app ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Information Collected

The App may collect the following types of information:

  • User Preferences: Stored locally using SharedPreferences (e.g., alarm settings, theme, notifications).
  • Device Information: Such as device model, OS version, screen size, language, and unique device identifier (used for crash reporting or analytics).
  • Internet Usage: The App may check internet connectivity and fetch remote configuration or content as needed.
  • Ad Interaction Data: Anonymous data collected by third-party ad providers to improve ad relevance and experience.
  • App Usage Data: Anonymous events (e.g., button clicks, screen visits) may be collected for analytics and user experience improvements.

Permissions

The App may request the following permissions, depending on features you use:

  • Internet: To fetch content, display ads, or check connectivity.
  • Camera: Required for features like QR scanning or photo capture.
  • Storage Access: To select images from the gallery or save content locally.
  • Notification Access: To deliver reminders, alarms, and important updates.

Permissions are requested only when required and can be managed in your device's system settings.

Use of Information

The collected information may be used to:

  • Provide and improve the App’s core features (e.g., alarms, reminders).
  • Store and remember your preferences.
  • Enhance app performance, stability, and personalization.
  • Support development through analytics and ad revenue.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Information may be shared only:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of users or the public.
  • With third-party services such as Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics (see Third-Party Services).

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services such as:

  • Google AdMob (ads and monetization)
  • Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics (analytics and crash reporting)

Notifications

The App may send local notifications to inform you of alarms, events, or other time-based reminders. You can control notification settings from within the App or your device.

Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or storage system is fully secure.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If a parent believes their child has provided personal data, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

User data is stored as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, or as required by law. Analytics and crash data are anonymized and retained for service improvement.

User Rights

International Data Transfers

If user data is transferred outside your country, we ensure appropriate legal and technical protections are applied.
